Our Story

A New Standard
of Nigerian Luxury

Chiben Leisure Hotels Limited stands as Anambra State's finest hospitality destination — a commercial, eco-friendly and post-modern building sheathed in aplitic stone and glass, featuring capsule elevators that rise through a landmark of refined architecture.

Situated at No. 41 Regina Caeli Road, off the Enugu-Onitsha Expressway in Awka, we cater to both the discerning business traveler and those seeking pure leisure in equal measure.
